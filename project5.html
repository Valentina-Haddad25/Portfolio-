As a Virtual Data Science Participant with the National Student Data Corps (NSDC) in collaboration with Columbia University and the U.S. Department of Transportation (DOT)/Federal Highway Administration (FHWA), I applied advanced data analysis techniques to derive meaningful insights from complex datasets. This role enabled me to strengthen my skills in data visualization, statistical analysis, and collaborative problem-solving within the context of real-world transportation data.

Objective:

The objective of this project was to analyze large datasets using the Team Data Science Process (TDSP) framework within Colab Notebooks. I aimed to uncover trends, visualize key insights effectively, and communicate data-driven findings in a concise and impactful manner through interactive presentations and poster boards.

Approach:

  • Data Analysis: Utilized TDSP Colab Notebooks to clean, process, and analyze transportation-related datasets, identifying patterns and anomalies to support data-driven decision-making.
  • Data Visualization: Designed and developed a one-page virtual poster board showcasing key insights using advanced visualization techniques, ensuring clarity and impact in data presentation.
  • Project Submission: Submitted final projects to the Northeast Big Data Innovation Hub and DOT/FHWA, highlighting analytical methodologies, data interpretations, and actionable recommendations.
  • Recognition: Earned a Data Science Certificate from Columbia University for successful project completion, with select visualizations featured on the NSDC website as part of a national showcase.
  • Public Presentation: Invited to present findings at a live event hosted by NSDC and DOT/FHWA, demonstrating strong analytical, communication, and public speaking skills.

Impact & Future Work:

This experience significantly enhanced my proficiency in data science methodologies, with a focus on data storytelling and effective communication of technical results to diverse audiences. Moving forward, I plan to expand my expertise in predictive analytics and machine learning, applying these skills to solve complex challenges in transportation systems, public policy, and beyond.
