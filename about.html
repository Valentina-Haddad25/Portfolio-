About Me

Hi, I'm Valentina Haddad—a creative technologist and storyteller passionate about bridging the gap between innovation and human connection. As a senior at Boston University double majoring in Computer Science and English, I thrive at the intersection of technology and the arts. From building AI-driven applications to creating video game narratives, I find inspiration in the blend of logic and creativity.

I am fueled by the belief that every project—be it coding, writing, or exploring—is an opportunity to connect, create, and contribute. I look forward to the journeys ahead, weaving stories and solutions, one line of code or page at a time.
